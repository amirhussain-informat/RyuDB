  Aggregates: both do COUNT/SUM/AVG/MIN/MAX + COUNT(*). DuckDB adds COUNT(DISTINCT), GROUP_CONCAT, MEDIAN, quantile, mode, statistical & order-sensitive aggs. RyuDB's COUNT(col) works only on the cuDF fallback
  path, not the fused kernel; no COUNT(DISTINCT).
